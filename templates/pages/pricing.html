Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after the 14-day trial?
After your trial ends, you'll be asked to add a payment method to continue. You won't be charged during the trial, and you can cancel anytime before it ends with no obligation.
Is there a limit on users?
No! The Pro plan is priced per company with unlimited users. Whether you have 5 or 50 employees, the price stays the same. We believe security education should scale with your team.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
Is SMBShield GDPR compliant?
Yes, we're fully GDPR compliant. We're built in Europe, for European businesses. All data is processed and stored in EU data centers, and we're transparent about how we use your data.
Do you offer discounts for annual billing?
Yes! Annual billing saves you 2 months. Instead of $99/month, you pay $990/year (equivalent to $82.50/month). Contact us to switch to annual billing.
